CRITICAL SAFETY INFORMATION

  • Shelters in **schools** are typically **ONLY open during non-school hours.**
  • **Activation:** Most shelters open when a **Tornado Watch** is issued.
  • **Pet Policies:** Policies vary. Check with the specific location about pets (Decatur City Hall allows leashed cats/dogs in crates).
